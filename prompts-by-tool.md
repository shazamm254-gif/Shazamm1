⚙️ TOOL TIPS
═══════════════════════════════════════

ImageFX:
• Set aspect ratio to Portrait BEFORE generating.
• Use the "expressive chips" (underlined words) to swap a style fast.
• Generates 4 images per run. Re-roll free.

Bing / DALL·E 3:
• Generates 4 per prompt; click each to download full-res.
• If a face looks off, add: "natural realistic face, symmetrical".
• For text shots, keep the quoted words SHORT or it misspells them.
• Crop to 9:16 afterward in CapCut (free).

Consistency trick (same eye in Shot 1 & 11):
• Generate Shot 1 first in ImageFX. Pick your favorite.
• For Shot 11, reuse the EXACT Shot 1 prompt + add ", washed in deep red light".
• Same lens + same wording keeps the look matching.
