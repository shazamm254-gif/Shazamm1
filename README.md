## 📱 Phone Workflow

1. **Generate 11 images** — free, no card. Use [ImageFX](https://labs.google/fx) for photoreal shots, [Bing Image Creator](https://www.bing.com/create) for graphic/text shots. Prompts are [below](#-image-prompts--by-tool).
2. **Crop to 9:16** if needed (Bing outputs square) — do it in CapCut.
3. **Drop images into CapCut** in timestamp order.
4. **Add on-screen text** from [this list](#-on-screen-text-timed) — bold condensed font (Anton / Bebas Neue), white + red.
5. **Voiceover** — record yourself, or paste the VO lines into a TTS app. Confident, slightly unsettling, plain-spoken.
6. **Upload** — paste the [caption + hashtags](#-caption--hashtags).

> ⚠️ Keep the disclaimer beat (7–9s) in. It keeps you credible and platform-safe.

## 🧪 Alternate Hooks (A/B test)

Swap the 0–2s line to test which retains best.

- **A — Direct callout:** "Most people fail this in the first five seconds. Including you, probably."
- **B — Provocative stat:** "One in twenty-five people is a sociopath and has no idea. Let's find out if it's you."
- **C — Mid-tension accusation:** "Don't lie to yourself on question three. That's where you'll give it away."

---

## ♻️ The Re-watch Payoff

Q1 reads as "what do you feel for the **stranger**" — but the ending rewind reveals it actually measured whether **you** noticed your *own* reaction at all. The "flinch" line only fully lands on a second viewing. That's the engine: the final line sends people back to the top, which spikes watch-time and drives "🩸 or ✅" comments.
