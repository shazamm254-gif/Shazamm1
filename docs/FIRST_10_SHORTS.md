# Project 3000 — Your First 10 Shorts (ready to produce)

Ten complete, ready-to-shoot Shorts across your four series. Each one is built
to win the first 2 seconds and loop or provoke a comment at the end.

**How to read each script:**
- **HOOK (0–2s)** — the exact words on screen + said aloud in the first frame. This is 80% of the battle.
- **VO** — the full voiceover narration, timed to ~15–30s. Narrate it slow, low, documentary-ominous — like a nature doc filmed from the year 3000.
- **ON-SCREEN** — big text cues (most people watch muted first).
- **VISUALS** — what to generate / show, cut every 1.5–3s.
- **END** — the loop line or question (drives re-watch + comments).
- **TITLE / DESC** — paste-ready packaging. Run through `tools/optimize_metadata.py` before posting.

Narration target: ~2.5 words/second. So a 20-second Short ≈ 50 words of VO. Keep it tight.

## Production notes

- **Narration voice:** slow, low, certain. Pauses sell the ominous tone more than words do. If using an AI voice, pick a deep documentary-style male or neutral voice and slow it ~10%.
- **Music:** one low, tense drone bed under everything for brand recognition. Same track across the series.
- **Caption/comment prompt:** end each post's caption with a question to farm comments, e.g. "Earth or Mars — which evolves the scarier human?"
- **Batch these.** Generate visuals for all 10 in one session, record all 10 VOs back to back, then schedule one per day. Don't produce them one at a time.
- **Validate packaging:** before each upload, run
  `python tools/optimize_metadata.py --title "<title>"` and fix any ✗ it flags.
