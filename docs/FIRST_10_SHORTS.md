# Cosmic Dread — Your First 10 Shorts (ready to produce)

Ten complete, ready-to-shoot Shorts across your four series. Each is built to win
the first 2 seconds and loop or provoke a comment at the end.

**How to read each script:**
- **HOOK (0–2s)** — exact words on screen + said aloud in frame 1. 80% of the battle.
- **VO** — full voiceover, timed to ~15–30s. Narrate it slow, low, and certain — a calm voice describing something almost too vast to comprehend.
- **ON-SCREEN** — big text (most people watch muted first).
- **VISUALS** — what to generate; cut every 1.5–3s.
- **END** — the loop line or question (drives re-watch + comments).
- **TITLE / DESC** — paste-ready. Run through `tools/optimize_metadata.py` before posting.

Narration target: ~2.5 words/second (a 20s Short ≈ 50 words). Keep it tight.
These facts are grounded in real astronomy — dramatized, not fabricated. Lean
into awe; don't invent numbers.

## 1 — "Falling Into a Black Hole" · *Space Horror*

- **HOOK (0–2s):** *"This is what happens if you fall into a black hole."*
- **ON-SCREEN:** INTO THE BLACK
- **VO:** "This is what happens if you fall into a black hole. To anyone watching, you slow down — and freeze at the edge, forever. - **END:** *"…and from outside, you're still falling."* (loops to the hook)
- **TITLE:** What happens if you fall into a black hole
- **DESC:** Time freezes, gravity stretches you into a thread. This is spaghettification. Subscribe for more cosmic dread. #space #universe #cosmos #astronomy #blackhole #shorts #cosmichorror

## Production notes

- **Voice:** slow, low, certain. Pauses sell the scale more than words do. If using an AI voice, pick a deep documentary tone and slow it ~10%.
- **Music:** one low, ominous drone/ambient bed under everything — same track across the series for instant brand recognition.
- **Visual style:** dark backgrounds, one glowing focal point, deep blues and blacks with the occasional violent flash. Keep the palette consistent so your channel grid reads as *yours*.
- **Caption/comment prompt:** end each post's caption with a question to farm comments, e.g. "Which one keeps you up at night — black holes or the Great Attractor?"
- **Batch these.** Generate all 10 sets of visuals in one session, record all 10 VOs back to back, schedule one per day. Never produce one at a time.
- **Validate packaging:** before each upload, run
  `python tools/optimize_metadata.py --title "<title>"` and fix any ✗ it flags.
