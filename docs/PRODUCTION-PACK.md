# Cosmic Dread — All-in-One Production Pack

Everything you need to make all 10 Shorts, in one file. No jumping around.
Works great pasted into Google Docs / Notes so you can produce from your phone.

## How to make one Short (the loop)

1. Pick a Short below.
2. **Generate the 4 shots** — copy each prompt block into your AI image or video
   tool (Midjourney, Flux, Leonardo, Runway, Kling…), set it to **vertical 9:16**,
   make 3–4 variations, keep the best. The style is already written into each
   prompt, so your clips all match.
3. **Record the voiceover** — read the VO slow, low, and certain.
4. **Edit** — lay the 4 shots over the voice, cut every 1.5–3 seconds, add one
   low ominous music track + on-screen captions.
5. **Post** — use the TITLE and DESCRIPTION provided. Reply to early comments.

Batch all 10 in one sitting, then schedule one per day. That's the whole job.

## Reusable settings (set once)

- **Voice:** deep, slow, documentary. Slow an AI voice ~10%. Same voice every time.
- **Music:** one low ominous drone under everything, same track across all videos.
- **Captions:** burn the hook + key words on screen, big and high-contrast, top third.
- **Pacing:** cut every 1.5–3 seconds. Length 20–35s.
- **Comment prompt:** end your caption with a question, e.g. "Which one keeps you up at night?"
- **Posting:** one per day. Reply to comments in the first hour.

Make all 10 in one batch, schedule them out, and just let them roll.
