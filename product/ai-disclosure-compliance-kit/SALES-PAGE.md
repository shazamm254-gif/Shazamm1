## FAQ

**Does labeling my content as AI hurt the algorithm/my reach?**
No — platforms have stated publicly that disclosure alone doesn't reduce
distribution. What actually gets penalized is *not* disclosing when it's
required.

**My channel is illustrated/stylized, not photorealistic — do I even need this?**
Probably not for most of your content, and the guide tells you exactly how
to confirm that in under a minute. But if you ever do a realistic
recreation, use a realistic AI voice/host, or touch a real event or person,
you'll need it — and this kit tells you exactly when that line gets
crossed.

**Is this legal advice?**
No. This is a practical, creator-facing framework for platform policy
compliance, not a substitute for a lawyer. If you're making political,
election, health, or financial content, or anything involving real
identifiable people, treat this as a starting point and confirm specifics
with current official policy (and legal counsel if the stakes are high).

**Will this go out of date?**
Platform policies do change. The kit is built around the *underlying
question* (could this be mistaken for something real?) rather than exact
menu screenshots, so it stays useful even as toggle names move — but always
sanity-check specifics against the platform's current help center before a
monetization-critical decision.
