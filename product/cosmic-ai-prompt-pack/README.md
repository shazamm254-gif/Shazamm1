**300+ ready-to-use AI prompts for making faceless cosmic / space-horror content** —
black holes, dying stars, rogue planets, cosmic voids, and the end of the
universe. Built for vertical (9:16) YouTube Shorts, but works anywhere.

Works with any modern AI image or video generator — Midjourney, Flux, Leonardo,
DALL·E, Runway, Kling, Pika, Sora-class tools.

## How to use it (the 30-second version)

1. Open `STYLE-GUIDE.md` and copy the **master style suffix**.
2. Pick a prompt from `IMAGE-PROMPTS.md` (or a video prompt from `VIDEO-PROMPTS.md`).
3. Paste: `[the prompt]` + `[master style suffix]` + your tool's aspect-ratio flag.
4. Generate 3–4 variations, pick the best, drop it into your edit.

Keeping the same style suffix on every prompt is the whole trick — it's what
makes your channel grid look like *one channel* instead of random space images.

> **Tip:** producing a specific Short? Skip straight to `SHOT-LISTS.md` — the
> prompts are already sequenced shot-by-shot for each of your first 10 videos.
