# Video / Motion Prompts — 60 cosmic clips

For AI video tools (Runway, Kling, Pika, Sora-class). Each prompt describes the
scene **and** the camera move — motion is what keeps a Short alive, so always
specify it. Append the master style cues from `STYLE-GUIDE.md` and set the tool
to a vertical 9:16 / portrait preset. Keep clips 3–6 seconds; cut between them
every 1.5–3s in your edit.

> Tip: slow, continuous camera moves (drift, push-in, orbit) read as cinematic.
> Avoid fast or jerky motion — it looks cheap and breaks the awe.
