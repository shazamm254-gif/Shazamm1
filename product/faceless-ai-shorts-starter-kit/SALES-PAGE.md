## FAQ

**Do I need to show my face or use my voice?**
No. The kit is built for fully faceless channels using AI visuals and an AI
voice (or your own narration if you prefer).

**Do I need to know how to code?**
No. The toolkit runs with simple copy-paste commands, and everything else is
plain guides and templates. (The tools are optional — the system works without
them.)

**Which AI tools do I need?**
Any AI image/video generator and any AI voice tool. The guide lists current
options for each layer; you pick one per layer.

**Will this work in my niche?**
Yes — the entire kit retunes to any niche by editing one config file. It's
strongest in visual, curiosity-driven niches (space, mythology, history,
science, mysteries).

**Is this beginner-friendly?**
Built for beginners. Day 1 to launch in the first week, with a 30-day plan after.
