# Mental Accounting — Short-Form Storyboard

**Topic:** Why a $20 bonus feels like guilt-free "fun money" but $20 from your paycheck feels like rent — the same $20.
**Voice:** Low-key, deadpan, dry, dead-serious underneath. Bar-friend energy, not a lecture.
**Format:** Voiceover over AI stills (pan/zoom). 35mm moody editorial look, amber/cash-green accent.

---

## Hook Alternates (pick one for 0–3s)

1. **(CHOSEN)** "A twenty-dollar bonus disappears and you don't feel a thing. The same twenty from your paycheck feels like rent money."
2. "Lose a twenty out of your wallet and you're annoyed for a day. Lose a twenty you 'found' last week — you don't even notice."
3. "Your brain has a setting that decides which of your dollars are real. You never get to see the switch."

---

## Final CTA Line (0:54–0:58)

> "Tell me what you blow your fun money on. I'm not judging. I'm taking notes."
